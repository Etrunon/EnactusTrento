WOODCARD

Sustainability in the palm of your hand

Electronic key cards and badges made from recycled wood and bioplastic, powered with eco-friendly technology. “Woodcard” allows you to get the same performance of PVC cards while considerably lowering the environmental impact of both production and disposal of the electronic badges, in the context of a circular economy.
Woocard badges can be used as: company badges for access and identification, key cards for electronic locks, badges for access and identification for every kind of activity and organization (Gyms, sport centers, canteens…)

Our Vision

A world free from the use of plastic in all those applications where it is not strictly necessary, populated by organizations proud of their role as ambassadors of a green and sustainable choice!

Our Mission

Provide companies, organizations and hotels with an alternative to polluting PVC electronic cards which aims at helping them in accelerating their transition towards a carbon neutral footprint. We eliminate plastic and substitute it with a product which brings new value to waste material and minimizes the impact of production and disposal.

Our Product

Our electronic cards are as flexible and resistant as PVC ones and are powered by an eco-friendly NFC technology. We can provide different NFC protocols, depending on the necessity of the client, and work together with them to develop a completely customized design for the cards.

Who are we?

“Woodcard” is a spin-off born from the international network of Enactus, an association present in 33 countries around the world where young university students develop and bring to the market entrepreneurial ventures with high social and environmental impact.
